Destiny 2 by Bungie

Destiny is a first person shooter game. This game has a variety things to offer such as a campaign story, missions, and a variety of different modes you can play with friends. The campaign storyline follows the story of a guardian who was the defender of the last city of humanity until it got destroyed. They strive to fight against the evil that has caused this outcome. The modes allows you to play a multiplayer versus with either friends or people online. You can also play as different characters, gain abilities, and unlock new gear to either compete in multiplayer or be of use within the campaign/missions.


PUBG by KRAFTON

PUBG is a online based multiplayer shooter game. Opposed to other multiplayer shooter games this one is strictly battlegrounds which means you compete with multiple people to be the last person standing and gaining the victory of that game. You can play solo or multiplayer to compete with other opponents. You can gain free based coins which allows you to open crates and also purchase items with real money. This game has multiple maps and gamemodes which allows the player to be engaged in a continuous state of enjoyment.


Apex Legends by Respawn Entertainment

Apex Legends is an online based multiplayer game. This game revolves around being battleground based which is very similar to Fortnite , which is another free game. Within game you are boarded on a ship and deploy to compete with a variety of different players to come out as the last person standing. This game includes rewards which can be gained through great performances within the multiplayer and also allows an online purchasing shop. This game also has a battlepass which can be completed through a one way purchase and allows the player to be more engage throughout the game to gain enough experience to fully complete it.
