Destiny 2 by Bungie

Destiny is a first person shooter game. This game has a variety things to offer such as a campaign story, missions, and a variety of different modes you can play with friends. The campaign storyline follows the story of a guardian who was the defender of the last city of humanity until it got destroyed. They strive to fight against the evil that has caused this outcome. The modes allows you to play a multiplayer versus with either friends or people online. You can also play as different characters, gain abilities, and unlock new gear to either compete in multiplayer or be of use within the campaign/missions.


Code of War by XDEVS LIMITED

Code of war is an online third person shooter game. The game is purposely for those who want to compete with other people in in order to be the best player within the global leaderboard. Within you are able to play with friends against an opposing team. You are also able to to gain items and advancements as you progress through the game which can help increase your chances to becoming the number one player of the global leaderboard. This game includes a variety of high aspects such as the realist mechanics such as the visuals and physics.
