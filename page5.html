Crossword Puzzles

Although some might find them boring, crossword puzzles are a great way to have fun and pass time. The premise of
the game is to solve words using the clues provided to you. As you discover words, you end up with some hints
for words you may be missing. Crossword puzzles are simple and easy to understand, but never get boring. If I really
had to be really critical about crossword puzzles, my only complaint is that some clues can be tricky to figure out
especially if you're not familiar with the puzzle's subject.

Angry Birds 2 is a free offline bird game. This game is about birds who fight pigs to save their eggs. They do this by launching themselves to the destroy the structures they may face and bring down the pigs.
